25-Week DSA Target Plan — Day by Day, Week by Week, Month by Month
🎯 The Mission
Go from wherever you are right now to interview-ready in 25 weeks.

Not "I've seen a lot of problems" ready.
Actually ready — where you can walk into a technical interview, get a medium you've never seen before, and solve it under pressure while explaining your thought process clearly.

25 weeks. No shortcuts. No skipped days. One lap at a time.

📊 The 25-Week Overview at a Glance
Month	Weeks	Phase	Focus
Month 1	Weeks 1–4	Foundation	Arrays, Strings, Hashmaps, Basic Sorting, Big-O
Month 2	Weeks 5–9	Core Patterns	Two Pointers, Sliding Window, Binary Search, Stacks, Queues
Month 3	Weeks 10–14	Intermediate Structures	Linked Lists, Trees, BSTs, Heaps
Month 4	Weeks 15–19	Advanced Topics	Graphs, Dynamic Programming, Backtracking
Month 5	Weeks 20–22	Deep Practice	Mixed problems, Weak spots, Timed sessions
Month 6	Weeks 23–25	Interview Simulation	Mocks, Company-tagged, Final sharpening
Total problems target: 375–425 problems across 25 weeks

📐 Daily Time Commitment
Day Type	Time	Problems
Learning day (new topic)	1.5–2 hours	2 problems + concept study
Practice day (known topic)	1–1.5 hours	2–3 problems
Revisit day	45 min–1 hour	2–3 revisit problems
Mock interview day	1.5 hours	2 problems under strict timer
Rest day (Sunday)	0–30 min	0 problems or light review only

🧠 Rules That Apply Every Single Week
No zero days. Even on rest days, a 5-minute mental review counts. A full skip doesn't.
Log everything. If it's not tracked, it didn't happen. Your tracker is your most valuable tool.
Revisits are sacred. A problem you looked up the answer for and never revisited is a wasted problem.
Speak out loud. From Week 10 onward, every problem should involve verbal explanation. Interviews are spoken, not silent.
Sleep matters. Your brain consolidates patterns during sleep. Cutting sleep to grind more problems is counterproductive.
Comparison is poison. Someone on Reddit solved 600 problems in 3 months. Good for them. Your journey is yours. 375 deeply understood problems beat 600 skimmed problems every time.

"25 weeks. 175 days. 375+ problems. Not to memorize solutions — but to build a mind that can solve anything."
